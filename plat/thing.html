Dark chocolate, one, and chopped;

Swirl with honey in the pot.

Lemon Slices, sliced and squeezed;

One poppy crushed and dropped.

Peppermint Candy, chopped for taste;

Rambutan juiced into the paste.

Drop a Robin Egg in, Crushed into four, 

Then a Star Anise, no ingredients more. 

 

Leave for 15 hours, 20 at most;

Then pour quickly for a potion to boast.
